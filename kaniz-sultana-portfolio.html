About Kaniz

Beauty, Education &
Purpose

I'm Kaniz Sultana — a professional makeup artist with over a decade of hands-on experience creating stunning looks for weddings, events, photoshoots, and television. My work is rooted in bringing out each client's natural beauty with care and precision.

Beyond the vanity, I run makeup training programs to empower aspiring artists — and I'm deeply committed to social welfare work, supporting women and communities across Bangladesh.
